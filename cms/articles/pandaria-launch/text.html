World of Warcraft's fourth expansion, Mists of Pandaria has launched to many eager fans of the game. As a follow up to 2010's Cataclysm, Pandaria takes a decidedly different direction, giving up earth shattering drama and a focus on the existing content, Pandaria is closer to the first two expansions. It adds the new continent of Pandaria and raises the level cap to 90, as well as introducing the new playable race, the Pandaren.

Our own Certify is playing through the new content and his impressions on the expansion are forthcoming, so stay tuned.
